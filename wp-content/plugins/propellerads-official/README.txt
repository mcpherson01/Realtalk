This plugin enables WordPress site owners (Publishers) to easily integrate and manage PropellerAds ad codes to increase revenue from their sites.

== Description ==

PropellerAds is one of the world’s biggest advertising networks, helping website owners, traffic sources, and networks monetize their global mobile and web inventory.

At PropellerAds we offer a variety of top-performing monetization tools including Push Notification, Interstitial, Native Banners, Smart Links and our flagship OnClick popunder.

The plugin enables WordPress site owners to easily increase revenue from their sites or blogs. You can start monetizing your audience in 3 easy steps:
- [Sign-up](https://publishers.propellerads.com/#/pub/auth/signUp) for a Publisher account (instant approval).
- Install & activate the plugin.
- Create and update your zone list to get active ad zones from your account. Start earning from each site visitor!
- If you have any questions, or need support, please contact us via email contact.us@propellerads.com.

= What is PropellerAds? =

PropellerAds is a network of over 150,000 publishers with the goal of generating more revenues from web and mobile traffic with the most effective monetization solutions in the industry.

Our robust optimization technology and auto-learning algorithms match relevant advertisers to every individual visitor to deliver only the highest paying ads on your sites.

By combining campaigns from thousands of direct advertisers and local ad networks, Propeller Ads provides the ultimate level of audience monetization and the highest revenue per visitor.

As a PropellerAds publisher, you can make money not only from your website, but also from your mobile apps, online games, widgets, social groups, software, or just sending any other kind of traffic.

Choose from a wide range of ad formats to find a balance between high revenues and protecting your users’ experience:

**Push Notifications** – exclusive monetization solutions suitable for any website. User-friendly and fully compliant with Google Adsense.These browser notifications take completely no space on your website, bring instant per-subscription revenue (CPS) and perfectly complement other ad formats. You will earn even if a user is not visiting your website anymore. Learn more on our site

**Onclick Ads (Popunder)** – a monetization powerhouse and one of our top performing ad formats in terms of revenue. These full-tab ads are user-initiated and appear in the new browser tab, allowing you to monetize every visitor. It is fully compatible with banners & AdSense.

**Native Banners** – content widgets that contain ads relevant to your website content. Control how the ads are displayed on your website and earn more money without ruining the user experience.

**Interstitial** – fast-loading, skippable ad units, displayed before or after specific website pages. These ads are perfect for additional monetization as they can be easily customized and offer high CTR.

**Smart Links** – monetize any traffic type with special URLs designed to automatically take visitors to the most relevant offers (thanks to smart rotation mechanism). The best solution for paid (pop), redirect, 404 traffic, toolbar, expired domains, etc.

To learn more about PropellerAds please [visit our website](https://propellerads.com/)

== Installation ==

1. [Sign up](https://propellerads.com/registration-publisher/) for Publisher account at PropellerAds. Registration is 100% free and takes fewer than five minutes.
2. Extract propellerads-official.zip to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
4. Use the PropellerAds screen to configure the ad rotation settings.

To find your Zone IDs, simply log in to your publisher account, click on Sites, and choose a website you want to display ads for. Zone IDs are located in the column “Channel” as shown on the screenshot below

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I find my Zone ID for Onclick / Interstitial ? =

To find your Zone IDs, log in to your publisher account, click on Sites, and choose a website you want to display ads for. Zone IDs are located in the column “Channel,” as shown on the screenshot below

If you don’t have Ad Zones yet, create them by clicking the “Add New Channel” button.

= Can I use PropellerAds ad formats on the same page with Google AdSense? =

Our monetization solutions are indeed fully compatible with all other networks, including AdSense.

= How do I stop ads from displaying to registered site users? =

If you want to disable ads for registered site users (including administrators) check the option “Disable ads for logged in users.”

= What are your traffic and website requirements? =

With a few rare exceptions, we accept all sites regardless of traffic volume. However, we do not accept websites containing adult content, malware, or any other content that violates our Terms and Conditions.

= Do your ad formats work on web sites in any language? =

We accept websites in any language and monetize traffic from more than 195+ countries & territories.

= How do I maximize my earnings? =

Revenue depends on your site traffic, its amount, and its quality. Some of the most important factors are: audience GEOs, share of mobile visitors, traffic sources, amount of page views per visitors, etc.

Increase your traffic, try different ad formats and you can be sure to get the best CPM rates in the industry.
